<p>
CoMap may be downloaded from <a href="http://download.gna.org/comap">http://download.gna.org/comap</a>.
The source code is available as a <code>.tar.gz</code> archive, and requires the Bio++ libraries Utils, NumCalc, SeqLib and PhylLib to be installed (see <a href="http://kimura.univ-montp2.fr/BioPP">http://kimura.univ-montp2.fr/BioPP</a>).
A static executable is also available for the Linux platform.
</p>
