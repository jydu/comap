.TH COMAP 1 LOCAL

.SH NAME

comap - Cosubstitution Mapping and coevolution detection

.SH SYNOPSIS

.B comap [options]

.SH AVAILABILITY

All UNIX flavors

.SH DESCRIPTION

comap performs (co) susbtitution mapping from a phylogeniy and asubstitution model, and detect groups of positions with significant coevolution.

.SH OPTIONS

You should refer to 'info comap' or to the online manual of comap for a complete list of available options.

.TP 5

--noninteractive

generates output for redirection in a file.

.TP

param=file

reads a file for loading options
